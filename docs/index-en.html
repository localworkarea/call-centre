Q&A

HOW QUICKLY DOES A CALL CENTER LAUNCH AN OUTSOURCING PROJECT?

The time to launch an outsourced call center project can vary depending on the scope of work and complexity of the project. Usually, after all the details are agreed upon, the necessary infrastructure and script are prepared, the launch process can take from a few days to several weeks. In most cases, the project can be launched in 3 business days after the terms of the contract are fulfilled.

WHAT WILL I GET FROM COOPERATION WITH YOU?

By working with us, you will receive qualified call center operators who will be able to optimize the process of processing customer requests, increase sales, and improve customer satisfaction. In addition, we provide recording of all conversations with customers stored in a reliable CRM system.

WHAT FUNCTIONS OF THE SALES DEPARTMENT CAN BE OUTSOURCED TO A CALL CENTER?

Some of the sales department functions that can be outsourced to a call center include handling incoming calls, lead generation, order processing, and technical support calls. For example, callback functions for customers, incoming call processing, outbound calls for active sales, customer support and problem solving, conducting questionnaires, surveys, etc. In addition, call center outsourcing can provide analytical reports and statistics on the work of the sales department.

HOW TO LISTEN TO THE OPERATOR'S CONVERSATION?

The National Operator company records all conversations with clients, which are stored in a secure CRM system so that you can find the history of each request, listen to the conversation, and check the quality of the work performed at any time.

CAN I ORDER CALL CENTER SERVICES OUTSIDE OF YOUR CITY?

Of course! Our call center in Ukraine has three branches in different cities, and you can contact the nearest branch. However, since we can easily perform all functions remotely, you can cooperate with us even from abroad.
